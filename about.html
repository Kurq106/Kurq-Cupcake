About Kurq Cupcakes

Kurq Cupcakes is a fictional bakery created as part of my web design project. The theme of the site is a fun, colorful cupcake shop that focuses on creativity, warmth, and a welcoming experience for visitors.

This site uses HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Bootstrap to practice building a responsive, modern layout. The pages feature images, videos, and interactive elements like an email signup section to demonstrate different web design techniques.
